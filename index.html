Our Focus

Research Areas

Our lab addresses critical questions about how prematurity disrupts brain development through three interconnected research programs.

1

Prematurity & Neurodevelopment

Combining human tissue analysis with mouse models to understand how early birth disrupts cerebellar development and identify therapeutic strategies.

2

NEC & Brain Injury

Investigating how necrotizing enterocolitis extends beyond the gut to cause selective vulnerability in the developing brainstem.

3

SIDS in Preterm Infants

Exploring how prematurity disrupts cerebellar-brainstem circuits essential for cardiorespiratory control and arousal responses.
